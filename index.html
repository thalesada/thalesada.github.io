Welcome!
I am currently a postdoctoral fellow in computational astrophysics at MPA in Munich, Germany. However, I will be moving to Princeton as a NASA Hubble and Lyman Spitzer Jr. fellow this fall.

My research interests include the nature of dark matter, the edge of galaxy formation and energetic feedback coupling from supernovae. To tackle these questions, I develop a galaxy formation model called LYRA within the framework of the cosmological hydrodynamic simulation code AREPO. It simulates galaxy formation and includes a multi-phase ISM, individual stars and resolved supernovae.

Get in touch with me if you are interested in collaborating!
